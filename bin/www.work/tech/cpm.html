CP Modifiers (CPM)

When calculating how much damage a Pokémon will do, Pokémon GO takes into account the Level of the Pokémon. It does this by multiplying the Pokémon's BaseAttack and Attack IV by a constant corresponding to the Pokémon's level. See Move Set Damage Formulas for more details.
