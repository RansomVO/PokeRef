Pokémon GO Tips

There are a lot of secrets that Niantic doesn't document, but have been found and shared by other trainers.
This page outlines a selection of those secrets.


Advanced Search Terms 
˅

When looking at the Pokémon in your box, there is the ability to search. This allows you to easily find Pokémon by species, or by the nickname you have given them.
But wait! There's more!!!

There are some secret terms and modifiers that can be used:

INPUT Description Examples Results
Text If a species name, Pokémon of that species. "Squirtle" Squirtles
If a Pokémon type, Pokémon of that type. "fire" Fire-type Pokémon E.G. Magmar, Flareon, etc.
If a nickname, Pokémon with that nickname. "Foxy" Pokémon with the nickname "Foxy"
If a sub-string, Pokémon whose species or nickname have that beginning. "Sun" Sunkerns, Sunfloras and Pokémon with nicknames like "Sunny"
Special Words Pokémon of a special category "evolve" Pokémon you have sufficient candies to evolve
"defender" Pokémon that are currently in gyms
"legendary" Pokémon that are designated as Legendary
Number ID of Pokémon "3" Pokémon with the ID 3 I.E. Venusaur
CP of Pokémon "cp100" Pokémon with a CP of 100
HP of Pokémon "hp42" Pokémon with an HP of 42
- Specifies a range. "1-10" Pokémon with IDs between 1 and 10
"cp3000-" Pokémon with a CP of 3000 or higher
"hp-20" Pokémon with an HP of 20 or lower
@ Move or Move Type "@ice" Pokémon with an Ice-type move E.G. Avalanche, Blizzard, etc.
"@bite" Pokémon with the move Bite
+ Expanded Family Results "+clefairy" Pokémon in the Clefairy family I.E. Clefairy, Clefable and Cleffa
"+jolteon" Pokémon in the same family as Jolteon. E.G. Eevee, Flareon, etc.
"+Foxy" Pokémon in the same family as the one named "Foxy"
"+flying" Pokémon in the a family that contains a member that is flying-type
E.G. It would include Magikarp since Gyrados (in the Magikarp family) are Flying-type.
, Merge Results "posion,steel" Pokémon that are either Poison-type or Steel-type. (Good against Fairies)
& Refine Results "evolve&+dragon" Pokémon that you can evolve that are in a family with a member that is Dragon-type

Eevee Evolving 
˅

There are "Easter Egg" tricks in Pokémon GO that will allow you to control what an Eevee will evolve into.

Naming Trick

The Naming trick allows you to specify what the Eevee will evolve into by renaming it before evolving.
THIS WILL ONLY WORK ONCE PER NAME!!! (Make sure you use it on a good Eevee!)

Process:

  1. Set the Nickname for the Eevee to the one desired from the table below.
  2. Evolve the Eevee.

Gen 2 Timing Trick

The Timing trick can be used to evolve the Eevee into the Gen 2 evolution you want.
BONUS: It can be used multiple times!

Process:

  1. Make the Eevee your buddy.
  2. Walk at least 10 Km with him as your buddy
    AND
    make sure he has found 2 Eevee Candies.
  3. While the Eevee is still your buddy, evolve it a the time desired from the table below.
