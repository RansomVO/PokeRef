Pokémon Name or ID: 
?
- Specifies a range.
Examples:
  • 1-10: All Pokémon with IDs between 1 and 10.
  • -3: All Pokémon with IDs 3 or lower.
  • 100-: All Pokémon with IDs 100 or higher.
+ Specifies a family.
Example:
  • +pidgey: All Pokémon in the Pidgey family.
    (I.E. Those that use Pidgey candies.)
, Specifies to match one criteria OR another.
Example:
  • 1-10,BB: All Pokémon with:
    IDs between 1 and 10
    - OR -
    a name that contains BB. (E.G. Krabby, Wobbeffet and Snubbull)
& Specifies one criteria AND another.
Example:
  • 1-10&B: All Pokémon with:
    IDs between 1 and 10
    - AND -
    a name that contains BB.
