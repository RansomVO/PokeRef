CP Modifiers (CPM)

When calculating how much damage a Pokemon will do, Pokemon GO takes into account the Level of the Pokemon. It does this by multiplying the Pokemon's BaseAttack and Attack IV by a constant corresponding to the Pokemon's level. See Move Set Formulas for more details.
